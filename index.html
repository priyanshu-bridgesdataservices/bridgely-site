Bridgely

Infrastructure for transactional, system, and demo communications across our internal and client applications.

Purpose

This domain is used to send transactional and system-triggered emails from development, staging, demo, and production environments. Examples include sign‑in links, verification codes, password resets, and workflow notifications.

Mail Characteristics

  • Audience: verified users, stakeholders, and test recipients.
  • Types: one‑to‑one notifications; no bulk marketing.
  • Initiation: user action or system event only.

Compliance & Handling

  • Unsolicited mail is not sent.
  • Bounce and complaint feedback are monitored via Amazon SES.
  • Test/demonstration sends are limited and rate‑controlled.

Contact

Email: support@bridgely.site

Policy Notice: Bridgely does not send unsolicited email. Messages are transactional, system‑generated, or sent to verified users for legitimate communication and demonstration purposes.
